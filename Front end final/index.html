About HelioSense AI

HelioSense AI is a complete AI-powered solar planning platform that analyzes rooftops using satellite imagery and intelligent algorithms to recommend the best solar panel placement, estimate energy generation, and calculate return on investment.

Whether you are a homeowner exploring solar for your residence or a business evaluating commercial installation, HelioSense AI empowers you to make intelligent, data-driven solar installation decisions — from rooftop suitability and panel layout to electricity bill analysis and financial forecasting.
